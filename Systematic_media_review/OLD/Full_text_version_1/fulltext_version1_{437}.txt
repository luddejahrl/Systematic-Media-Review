Event

Dozens of human rights activists have gathered in the vicinity of the Brook House Immigration Removal Centre near Gatwick Airport (LGW) June 12 to protest government plans to deport refugees to Rwanda from June 14. Demonstrators have blocked a section of Charlwood Road outside of the Brook House facility. No flight disruptions were immediately reported at LGW in connection with the rally.

Heightened security and localized ground transport disruptions are likely around the scene of the protest over the coming hours.

Advice

Avoid the protest. Consider taking alternative routes to circumvent any related disruptions; allow extra time for travel to LGW June 12. Adhere to all instructions issued by local authorities.
