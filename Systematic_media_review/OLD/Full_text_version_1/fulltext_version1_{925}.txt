No breakthrough at India-China diplomatic dialogue

newsare.net

In a bid to restore remaining issues in eastern Ladakh, India and China will hold the next round of military-level talks at an early date to create conditions for the restoration of normalcy in the bilateral ties, the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) said.
