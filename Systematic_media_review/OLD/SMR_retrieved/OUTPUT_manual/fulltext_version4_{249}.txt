At least two children died this morning while hundreds of others were left homeless as floods from the Virunga Mountain continue to hit the Northern and Western Provinces.

The NewTimes journalist says 8-year-old boy died from Musanze District. A 12-year-old boy from Nyabihu District has also died.Floods resulting from heavy rains since Tuesday this week in Northern and Western Provinces have left several people homeless.

Several sectors in Musanze District and other surrounding areas have also been seriously affected.
