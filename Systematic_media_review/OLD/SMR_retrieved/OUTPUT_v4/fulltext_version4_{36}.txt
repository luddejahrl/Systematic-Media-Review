RUHANGO-A fatal accident involving a Fuso truck has killed at least 8 people in Ruhango sector, Ruhango District.Another 15 were injured and were immediately rushed at various nearby health centers, including Ruhango, Kibingo and Kigoma.One of the injured, who was in critical conditions, was taken to Kabgayi Hospital, Muhanga District.The accident occurred in the rural village of Rebero, Gikoma cell, Ruhango sector on Wednesday at around 7pm, according to Police.
