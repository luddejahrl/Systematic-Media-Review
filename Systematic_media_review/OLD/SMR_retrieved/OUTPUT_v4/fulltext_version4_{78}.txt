NYANZA- AT LEAST four policemen were killed when a speeding truck rammed into their Toyota pickup in Mukingo sector, Nyanza district, today morning.

The officers died on the spot, Supt. Hubert Gashagaza, the southern regional police spokesperson, told The New Times.

Four other police officers and the driver of the ill-fated pick-up number RNP 053N, were also seriously injured. They were by press time being treated at King Faisal Hospital, Kigali, and at the University Teaching Hospital of Butare.
