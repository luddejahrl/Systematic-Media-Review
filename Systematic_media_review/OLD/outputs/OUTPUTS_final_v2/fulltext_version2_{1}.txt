Kigali, Feb 3 (Xinhua) Landslides triggered by heavy downpour on Sunday left 13 people dead two others injured in different parts of Rwanda, the Ministry of Emergency Management said Monday, warning that the death toll could rise. Majority of those killed were from the capital Kigali's Kicukiro, Gasabo, and Nyarugenge
Districtcts, Olivier Kayumba, the Permanent Secretary in Rwanda's Ministry of EmergencyManagement told reporters in a news briefing. In one of the incidents, a landslide destroyed a house in Kanombe sector, Kicukiro district, Killing three family members, a mother and two children, he said.
